The most common symptoms of COVID-19 are :-

  • Fever
  • Nasal congestion
  • Sore throat
  • Fatigue
  • Dry cough

Though COVID can affect any person of any age group. People aged 60 years and over, and those with underlying medical problems like high blood pressure, heart and lung problems, diabetes, obesity or cancer, are at higher risk of developing serious illness.

People with symptoms of COVID and the people who have been in contact with the COVID infected person must get tested for COVID.
